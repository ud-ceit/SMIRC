Welcome

The Student Multidisciplinary Innovation and Research Conference (SMIRC) is an annual academic platform that highlights the creativity, innovation, and research excellence of undergraduate and graduate students across diverse disciplines. The conference brings together students, faculty, researchers, and industry professionals to explore the intersections between Computing and Information Systems, Engineering, Business, Law, and Education. Each edition of SMIRC includes two sessions per year, organized at the end of each academic semester (Fall and Spring), offering continuous opportunities for students to present their work, exchange ideas, and engage in interdisciplinary collaboration.

This year, under the theme “Innovating Across Disciplines for a Smarter Future,” SMIRC promotes cross-disciplinary research, applied innovation, and problem-solving that address contemporary societal and technological challenges. Through poster sessions, and live demonstrations, participants will showcase projects that reflect emerging trends in artificial intelligence, sustainability, digital transformation, and data-driven decision-making.
The conference also features expert panels and innovation challenges led by academic and industry leaders. These sessions aim to nurture a culture of creativity, strengthen university–industry partnerships, and align student research with national priorities in artificial intelligence, sustainability, and smart development. Sponsored by the Research Office, in collaboration with the IEEE UAE Section and participating colleges, SMIRC seeks to inspire a culture of inquiry, collaboration, and lifelong learning across all academic disciplines.

The first session of SMIRC 2025 will be held on November 29, 2025, at the Academic City Campus, Dubai.

Call For Papers

Submissions are invited from undergraduate and graduate students across all disciplines, including but not limited to:

  • Computing and Information Systems: Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Cybersecurity, Cloud Computing, Internet of Things, and Smart Applications.
  • Engineering and Technology: Robotics, Renewable Energy, Embedded Systems, Smart Cities, and Sustainable Design.
  • Business and Management: Innovation Management, Digital Transformation, Entrepreneurship, and FinTech.
  • Law and Policy: Cyber Law, Digital Governance, Data Privacy, and Legal Aspects of Emerging Technologies.
  • Education and Social Sciences: Technology-Enhanced Learning, Educational Innovation, and Social Impact Research.

Paper Submission Instructions

  • Position papers:  8-page limit, describing a position statement on issues related to the topics of the workshop. Position papers would lead to interesting discussions by raising key questions, controversial points of view, challenges, and ideas to address the identified issues.
  • Research papers:  12-page limit, describing original and novel research work, including research results and evaluations. The papers may present the ongoing research, novel ideas with preliminary results, advances of the state of the art, analysis of the current results in the state of the art or state of the practice, or experience in implementing the theories.
  • Industry Papers:  12-page limit, This track will be an opportunity for people associated with industry and academia to present their novel ideas while attracting funding and/or further expanding collaborations. An important criterion for papers to be submitted to the Industry Track is a clear industrial association, either in the form of an extensive evaluation of research results in industrial use cases or active participation of industry representatives as co-authors.
  • Papers should be formatted according to Springer’s LNCS Formatting Guidelines. Submissions must be in English and must not exceed the above-mentioned limit pages (including abstract, figures and references). Each paper must be submitted on or before the provided deadlines. The final submission should be formatted according to Springer’s LNCS Camera-ready instructions. Each paper will be reviewed by at least three reviewers.
  • All papers must be submitted electronically, as PDF file, to the Workshop Submission System: https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=icsoc2025
